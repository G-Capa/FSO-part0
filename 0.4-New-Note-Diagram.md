# 0.4-New-Note-Diagram

Exercise number 0.4 from Full Stack Open course by University of Helsinki

Small diagram depicting the situation where the user creates a new note on the page https://studies.cs.helsinki.fi/exampleapp/notes by writing something into the text field and clicking the ***Save*** button.
